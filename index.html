Community Info

Abuja is the capital city of Nigeria, known for its vibrant culture and economic growth opportunities for businesses and entrepreneurs alike.Abuja is Nigeria's planned capital, centrally located in the Federal Capital Territory (FCT), known for its modern infrastructure, green spaces, and status as the seat of government, replacing Lagos in 1991 to foster unity with its neutral ethnic and religious location. Key features include the Aso Rock monolith, Aso Rock Presidential Villa, National Assembly, National Mosque, National Christian Centre, and a cooler climate, making it a hub for politics, business, and diverse cultures.
