Site Name

Pasima Crypto Trading

Reason: This name clearly communicates the focus of the website on cryptocurrency trading, while "Pasima" adds a unique and memorable brand element.

Site Purpose

The Pasima Crypto Trading website serves as a comprehensive platform for cryptocurrency enthusiasts and traders. It provides real-time market data, trading tools, educational resources, and a user-friendly interface for executing trades across various cryptocurrencies.

Scenarios

1. As a new cryptocurrency investor, how can I learn about different trading strategies and risk management?

2. I'm an experienced trader looking for advanced charting tools. Does Pasima offer customizable technical analysis features?

Color Scheme

Primary Color: #1a5f7a (Deep Blue) - Used for headings, navigation, and call-to-action buttons

Secondary Color: #e1f1f9 (Light Blue) - Used for background accents and information boxes

Text Color: #333333 (Dark Gray) - Used for body text

Typography

Headings: Roboto, sans-serif - Bold weight for emphasis

Body Text: Arial, sans-serif - Regular weight for readability

Accent Text: Montserrat, sans-serif - Used for buttons and important callouts
